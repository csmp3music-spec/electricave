## Period Boston Asset Notes

This folder collects added source assets for a 1920s-30s Boston street railway look.

All texture sets below are from Poly Haven and are licensed CC0:

### Historical fit

Closest period fit right now:

- Boston `Type 5` and `PCC` remain the most relevant rolling stock already in the repo.
- The added tram models are best treated as temporary stand-ins until a BERy-specific car body is sourced or built.
- The downloaded textures are directly useful for 1920s-30s Boston street surfaces, masonry, and subway finishes.
